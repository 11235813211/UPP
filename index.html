What is UPP?

A small quirky group of STEM-loving students that think studying ridiculously challenging math problems is beyond fun.

In UPP we both prep for the Putnam Exam and self-teach advanced math!

Putnam Exam? Huh?

Unlike "normal" math tests, the Putnam Exam challenges you based off of problem-solving creativity, instead of classes taken and formulas memorized.

It's regarded as the hardest undergraduate math exam.

Why should I join?

Challenging yourself to these crazy problems is an extreme and rewarding way to develop your problem-solving skills.

And let's not forget the laughs we'll have together!
